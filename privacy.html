Privacy Policy for Tec.pi (Titan Elite Commerce)

Last Updated: November 2025

We at Tec.pi respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data we collect from you. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use the platform.

1. Information We Collect

Since the platform relies on the Pi Network ecosystem, we primarily collect the following information:

  • Pi Identity Data: When using Pi Login, we receive your Pi Username and Account ID to create and verify your account.
  • Transaction Data: We record details of purchases and sales (service value, transaction date) necessary for processing fees and resolving disputes.
  • Technical Data: Information about your browser, device, and IP address for security and service improvement.
Note: We absolutely DO NOT store or have access to your private key (Private Key) for your Pi Wallet.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate the Marketplace: To enable you to log in, list services, and complete transactions.
  • Security and Dispute Resolution: To verify identities and investigate complaints between the Seller and the Buyer.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze platform usage patterns and enhance the user experience.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Pi Network: To facilitate account authentication and payments via Pi Wallet.
  • The Counterparty: Your Pi Username is shared with the other party in the transaction (Seller/Buyer) to complete the service and communication.

4. Data Protection

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data. As financial transactions are handled primarily through the secure Pi Wallet, we rely on the security protocols provided by the Pi Network.

5. Changes to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

Privacy Policy for Commerce.pi (Physical Goods Marketplace)

Last Updated: November 2025

We are committed to protecting the data of our Pioneers. This policy details how Commerce.pi handles personal information, especially concerning physical product transactions.

1. Information We Collect

In addition to the standard Pi Identity Data (Username, Account ID), we collect the following mandatory information for physical goods transactions:

  • Shipping and Delivery Data: Full legal name, complete shipping address (street, city, country, postal code), and primary contact number. This is essential for successful delivery.
  • Transaction Records: Details of purchases, product specifications, and fulfillment status for escrow and dispute resolution.
  • Communication Data: Records of direct messages exchanged between the Buyer and Seller on the platform, used solely for resolving disputes.
Reminder: We absolutely DO NOT store or access your Pi Wallet Private Key.

2. Sharing of Information

Your privacy is paramount, but sharing certain data is necessary for logistics:

  • Sharing with the Counterparty: When a sale is made, the Buyer's full shipping details (Name, Address, Contact Number) are shared with the Seller to enable shipping and order fulfillment.
  • Logistics Partners: Shipping details are shared with third-party logistics companies (e.g., DHL, FedEx) responsible for delivering the product.
  • Legal and Pi Network Compliance: We may share data if required by law or to comply with the Pi Network's terms and security protocols.

3. Data Security and Retention

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the physical address and contact details you provide. Shipping data is retained for a maximum of 18 months post-delivery to facilitate any necessary warranty or fraud investigations.

4. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to request access to the data we hold about you and to request correction of any inaccurate personal information.
